--- title: Theorem 8.3 Parent: powerSeries8 ---
Given a double sequence $\{a_{ij\}, i=1,2,3,\cdots, j=1,2,3,\dots$ suppose that $$\sum\limits_{j=1}^{\infty} \abs{a_ij} = b_i, i=1,2,3,\dots$$ and $\sum b_i$ converges. Then $$\summ_{i=1}^{\infty} \summ_{j=1}^{\infty} a_{ij} = \summ_{j=1}^{\infty} \summ_{i=1}^{\infty} a_{ij}$$
Let $E=\{x_0, x_1, x_2, \dots \}$ and suppose $x_n \to x_0$ as $n \to \infty$.
