--- title: Theorem 8.3 parent: powerSeries08 order: 3 ---
Given a double sequence $\{a_{ij}\}, i=1,2,3,\cdots, j=1,2,3,\dots$ suppose that $$\sum\limits_{j=1}^{\infty} \abs{a_{ij}} = b_i, i=1,2,3,\dots$$ and $\sum b_i$ converges. Then $$\Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \Sum_{j=1}^{\infty} a_{ij} = \Sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} a_{ij}$$
Consider a countable set $E=\{x_0, x_1, x_2, \dots \}$. Let $x_0$ be limit point of $E$. Then $x_n \to x_0$ as $n \to \infty$. Define $$\begin{align} f_{i} (x_n) &= \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} \quad{i,n = 1,2,3,\cdots} \\ \text{Then } f_i (x_0) &= \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} a_{ij} \qquad{i=1,2,3,\cdots} \end{align}$$ \begin{align} \abs{f_i(x_n)} &= \abs{\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_{ij}} \\ &\leq \sum_{i=1}^{n} \abs{a_{ij}} \\ &\leq \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \abs{a_{ij}} \\ &= b_i\\ \abs{f_i(x_0)} &= \abs{\sum_{i=1}^{\infty} a_{ij}} \\ &\leq \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \abs{a_{ij}} \\ &= b_i \end{align}

Hence $\abs{f_i(x)} \leq b_i$ for any $x \in E$. Hence, by {% a '7.10' %}, $\sum\limits_{i=1}^{\infty} f_i(x)$ converges uniformly on $E$.

Let $g_m(x) = \Sum_{i=1}^{m} f_i (x)$ for $x \in E$.

Then $\lim\limits_{m \to \infty} g_m(x) = \Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} f_i (x)$ for $x \in E$.

Since the series $\Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} f_i(x)$ converges uniformly, the sequence of its partial sums $\{ g_m(x) \}$ converges uniformly on $E$. Also $x_0$ is a limit point of $E$. Hence by {% a 7.11 %},

$$\begin{align} \lim_{m \to \infty} \lim_{t \to x_0} g_m(t) &=\lim_{t \to x_0} \lim_{m \to \infty} g_m(t) \\ \lim_{m \to \infty} \lim_{n \to \infty} g_m(x_n) &=\lim_{n \to \infty} \lim_{m \to \infty} g_m(x_n) \\ \lim_{m \to \infty} g_m(x_0) &=\lim_{n \to \infty} \Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} f_i(x_n) \\ \Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} f_i(x_0) &=\lim_{n \to \infty} \Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \Sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{ij} \\ \sum\limits_{i=1}^{\infty} \sum\limits_{j=1}^{\infty} a_{ij} &=\lim_{n \to \infty} \Sum_{j=1}^{n} \Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} a_{ij} \\ \Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} \Sum_{j=1}^{\infty} a_{ij} &= \Sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \Sum_{i=1}^{\infty} a_{ij} \end{align}$$
